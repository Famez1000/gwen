Cope:
In the cope section, there are exercises to cope with/survie the anxxiety when it disturbs you. The best approach is to distract your mind as much as possible. Exercises include:
Draw and guess: interactive and fun
Joke
Leaf exc
Play your favorite music
Listen to the right meditation
Do bubble pop
Breathing exercises
Remember who you are
Vanquish with Gwyn AI to at least make it through the day.

Understand:
Read the FAQ about what anxiety is.
Find out when, in what situation you feel anxious.
Write down what you feel, where in your body (Body Signals).
It it chronic? Or just when a particular situation arises (Triggers)? (like speaking in public)
Ask yourself what the reason for the feeling is.
Check your thoughts and Patterns.
Understand with Gwyn AI to help you understand better


Heal:
Know what the problem is -> comes out of the understand screen.
Suppose you're afraid of stepping into an airplane, heal it by using logic, convince your mind that what you think is not factual.
Observe your thoughts throughout the day and catch yourself thinking negative thoughts.
Read what people say on internet about healing anxiety.
Accept what needs to be accepted, no matter what it is.
Forgive what needs to be forgiven
Let go what needs to be released.
Ask in meditation yourself which step to take next.
Catch yourself and try to avoid entering survival mode.
