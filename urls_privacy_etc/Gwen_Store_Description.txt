Gwen is a gentle anxiety support app designed to help you calm your body, steady your thoughts, and feel less alone when anxiety rises.

Whether you are feeling panicked, overwhelmed, tense, restless, or simply need a quiet moment, Gwen gives you practical tools you can use right away. The app combines breathing exercises, grounding techniques, journaling, mood check-ins, progress tracking, calming reflections, and supportive AI-powered guidance from Gwen.

Gwen is here to help you pause the spiral and come back to the present moment.

Features include:

• Chat with Gwen
Talk to Gwen when anxiety feels loud. Gwen offers warm, practical support, grounding suggestions, and gentle encouragement.

• Breathing exercises
Use calming breathing patterns to slow down, relax your body, and create space between you and anxious thoughts.

• Journaling and reflection
Write about your day, track your anxiety score, and use Gwen to summarize your journal entries into kind, useful reflections.

• Mood and anxiety tracking
Log how you feel, notice patterns over time, and see your progress through simple, supportive insights.

• Grounding tools
Use sensory grounding exercises to reconnect with your surroundings when anxiety feels intense.

• Reminders
Set gentle local reminders to check in, breathe, reflect, or take a calming moment.

• Progress insights
Review breathing sessions, calm streaks, anxiety logs, and reflections so you can notice what helps.

• Calming activities
Explore supportive exercises, affirmations, light distractions, and small moments of relief when your mind needs a reset.

Gwen is designed to feel warm, simple, and human. It does not shame you for feeling anxious. It does not ask you to “just calm down.” Instead, it gives you small next steps: breathe, notice, write, ground, reflect, and keep going.

Important: Gwen is not a medical device, therapist, crisis service, or replacement for professional care. If you feel in immediate danger, may harm yourself or someone else, or need urgent help, contact local emergency services or a trusted person right away.

Gwen is your pocket companion for anxious moments, quiet reflection, and daily emotional support.
