Gwyn info page
==============

Recommended page URL
--------------------

Recommended page title
----------------------
Gwyn

Recommended headline
--------------------
Your pocket companion for anxious moments.

Recommended intro text
----------------------
Gwyn is designed to help you manage anxiety with warm, practical support. The app brings together breathing exercises, grounding techniques, journaling, mood check-ins, reminders, progress tracking, calming activities, and supportive AI-powered guidance.

Gwyn does not tell you to "just calm down." Instead, it guides you through small, manageable steps: breathe, notice, write, ground yourself, reflect, and keep moving forward.

Section: What Gwyn helps with
-----------------------------
Breathing exercises
Use calming breathing patterns to slow your breathing, relax your body, and create space between yourself and anxious thoughts.

Journaling and reflection
Write about your day, track your anxiety level, and let Gwyn turn your journal entries into kind, helpful reflections.

Mood and anxiety tracking
Record how you feel, notice patterns over time, and follow your progress through simple, supportive insights.

Grounding tools
Use sensory grounding exercises to reconnect with the present moment when anxiety feels overwhelming.

Gentle reminders
Set reminders to check in with yourself, breathe, reflect, or simply take a calming moment.

Progress insights
Review your breathing sessions, calming streaks, anxiety logs, and reflections to discover what helps you most.

Calming activities
Explore supportive exercises, affirmations, gentle distractions, and soothing activities whenever your mind needs a reset.

Chat with Gwyn
Talk with Gwyn when anxiety feels overwhelming. Gwyn offers warm, practical support, grounding suggestions, and gentle encouragement.

Section: A simple way to begin
------------------------------
Open Gwyn when you feel anxious, unsettled, or overloaded. Choose a breathing exercise, write a quick journal entry, ground yourself in the present moment, or check in with how you feel. Over time, Gwyn helps you understand what supports you best.
