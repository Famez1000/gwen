Gwyn Privacy Policy Last update: July 1, 2026 Gwyn is an anxiety support app designed to help you calm down, reflect, journal, practice breathing and grounding exercises, track your progress, and receive supportive guidance from Gwyn. Your privacy matters. This Privacy Policy explains what information Gwyn may collect, how it is used, and what choices you have. Gwyn is not a medical, psychiatric, therapeutic, or emergency service and does not replace professional care. 1. Information Gwyn Stores on Your Device Gwyn stores most app information locally on your device. This may include: - Your name or nickname, if you choose to enter one. - Anxiety logs, mood check-ins, reflection answers, journal entries, and anxiety scores. - Breathing session counts, calm streaks, progress notes, and Gwyn progress analyses. - Custom affirmations and grounding objects you save. - Reminder settings and notification preferences. - App preferences such as theme, sound, haptic feedback, onboarding status, and accepted disclaimers. - Emergency contact name and phone number, if you choose to save one. - Favorite song or personal reality-check text, if you choose to save them. - Recent Gwyn jokes or similar app-generated content used to avoid repetition. This information is stored on your device using local app storage. Gwyn does not operate a user account system for this data, and we do not use our own server to collect or store your journal entries, anxiety logs, reminders, emergency contact, or profile preferences. If you uninstall the app or clear the app's data, locally stored information may be deleted. 2. Information Sent When You Use Gwyn AI Features Some Gwyn features use Google's Gemini AI service to generate supportive responses, summarize journal entries, analyze progress, guess drawings, or create light anxiety-relief content. When you choose to use these AI features, the information needed for that request may be sent to Google's Gemini API. This may include: - The message you type to Gwyn. - The page or feature context from which you opened Gwyn. - Journal entries or anxiety scores you choose to summarize or analyze. - Progress information used to create a reflection. - A drawing image, if you ask Gwyn to guess your drawing. - Recent generated jokes, if needed to avoid repeating similar jokes. Only use Gwyn AI features with information you are comfortable sending to a third-party AI service. Do not include highly sensitive personal information unless you want it processed for the response. Google may process this information according to its own terms and privacy practices for Gemini API services. Gwyn uses the information sent to Gemini only to provide the requested response in the app. 3. Notifications Gwyn can send local reminders if you enable them. Reminder times and related settings are stored on your device. Gwyn uses your device's notification system to deliver these reminders. Gwyn may ask for notification permission. You can enable, disable, or change notification permissions in your device settings. 4. Internet Access Gwyn requests internet access so that AI-powered Gwyn features can communicate with Google's Gemini API. Some external links, such as a favorite song link you choose to open, may also use your device's browser or another app. Gwyn does not use internet access to sell your personal information. 5. Payments and Purchases If Gwyn offers paid features through Google Play, payment processing is handled by Google Play. Gwyn does not collect or store your credit card number, debit card number, or full payment details. Google may provide purchase status information needed to unlock paid features. Google's handling of payment information is governed by Google's own privacy policy and payment terms. 6. How We Use Information Gwyn uses information stored on your device or provided to AI features to: - Provide anxiety support tools and app features. - Personalize Gwyn's responses and supportive guidance. - Save your progress, reminders, preferences, journal entries, and reflections. - Help you review patterns in anxiety scores, breathing sessions, and progress. - Generate AI responses, summaries, jokes, drawing guesses, and reflections when you request them. - Improve your experience inside the app. - Maintain app functionality, safety, and reliability. Gwyn does not use your personal information to diagnose you, provide medical treatment, or make clinical decisions. 7. Sharing of Information Gwyn does not sell your personal information. Gwyn may share information only in these limited situations: - With Google Gemini API when you choose to use AI-powered Gwyn features. - With Google Play if purchases or subscriptions are offered through Google Play. - With service providers necessary to operate app features, if such services are added in the future. - If required by law, legal process, or to protect rights, safety, or security. Most personal content you enter into Gwyn remains on your device unless you choose to use a feature that sends it to an external service, such as Gemini. 8. Analytics and Advertising At the time of this policy, Gwyn does not use third-party advertising networks and does not sell personal data for advertising. If analytics, crash reporting, or advertising tools are added in the future, this Privacy Policy should be updated to describe what is collected and how it is used. 9. Data Retention Locally stored information remains on your device until you delete it, clear the app's data, or uninstall Gwyn. Information sent to third-party services, such as Google's Gemini API or Google Play, is handled according to those services' own retention practices. 10. Your Choices You can control your information in several ways: - You can choose not to enter optional personal information. - You can edit or delete saved app content where the app provides that option. - You can clear Gwyn's app data through your device settings. - You can uninstall Gwyn to remove locally stored app data from your device. - You can disable notifications in Gwyn or in your device settings. - You can avoid AI-powered features if you do not want content sent to Gemini. - You can avoid opening external links if you do not want to leave the app. 11. Children Gwyn is intended for users who are old enough to use anxiety support and journaling tools responsibly. Gwyn is not directed to young children. If you are under the age required by your country or region to use apps like Gwyn without parental consent, please use Gwyn only with permission from a parent or legal guardian. 12. Health and Safety Gwyn provides general anxiety support, grounding suggestions, journaling prompts, breathing exercises, and supportive AI conversation. Gwyn does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, therapy, crisis care, or emergency services. If you feel in immediate danger, might harm yourself or someone else, or need urgent support, contact local emergency services or a trusted person right away. If you have medical symptoms that are severe, unusual, or concerning, contact a qualified medical professional. 13. Security Gwyn uses local device storage for most app data. The security of locally stored information also depends on your device security settings, such as your screen lock, operating system, backups, and app permissions. No method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure. Please be careful about the personal information you choose to enter into Gwyn or send to AI features. 14. International Processing If you use AI-powered Gwyn features, information may be processed by Google services in countries outside your own. Privacy protections may differ depending on where processing occurs. 15. Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last update" date at the top of this policy. You should review this policy periodically to stay informed about how Gwyn handles information. 16. Contact If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or Gwyn's handling of information, please use the developer contact details provided on Gwyn's Google Play Store listing.
